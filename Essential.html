WORKOUT
ESSENTIALS AND
PURPOSE.

BAG


Keeps all your gym gear organized and easy to carry. Helps you prepare everything you need before leaving home.

WATER BOTTLE


Staying hydrated boosts performance and prevents fatigue. Helps regulate body temperature and replace fluids lost through sweat.

SHOES


Provides proper support and grip during workouts. Prevents blisters and provide extra cushioning.

PROTEIN SNACKS


Refuels muscles after a workout. Helps with muscle recovery and growth, especially after lifting.

HEADPHONES


Keeps you motivated with music. Helps you focus by blocking distractions around you.

DEODORANT


Keeps you fresh and confident before, during, and after workouts. Prevents body odor caused by sweating.

SWEAT TOWEL


Wipes off sweat to stay clean and comfortable. Helps keep gym equipment hygience for everyone.

ANKETS


Used for lleg orglute exercises on cable machines. Adds resistance and improves lower-body strength.

HAIRBRUSH or HAIR TIES


Keeps your hair neat and out of your face while exercising, especially for girls.
